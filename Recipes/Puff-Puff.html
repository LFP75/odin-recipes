Puff-Puff

Description

This exquisite treat is perfect for either a delightful snack or elegantly presented as a sumptuous dessert!

This recipe is an absolute breeze to follow, using ingredients you probably already have right in your own kitchen.

Ingredients

    All purpose flour
  • Sugar
  • Yeast
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Nutmeg or Cinnamon

Steps

  1. In a large bowl combine all dry ingredients.
  2. Combine wet and dry ingredients.
  3. Cover the bowl and let sit for 1-2 hours.
  4. Heat oil and deep fry till golden brown.
