Suya

Description

Beef suya is a popular West-African street food of skewered and grilled beef.

It is known for its very spicy flavour

Ingredients

  • Thinly sliced beef
  • Suya spice blend
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Sliced onions and tomatoes

Steps

  1. Marinate the beef in the suya mix and vegetable oil.
  2. Skewer beef into wooden sticks.
  3. Grill until it is cooked and slightly charred.
